B — Refined Swiss

Grid-driven but with one accent color (Swiss red). Inter typeface. Slightly warmer background. The grid does the heavy lifting, color marks function only.

What both share (vs. current design)

Strip away:

  • Paper texture overlay
  • Glass morphism / backdrop blur
  • Gradient accent lines
  • Card shadows and hover lifts
  • Decorative animations
  • 4 font families → 1-2 max
  • Warm cream background

Introduce:

  • Mathematical grid (fixed left column)
  • Rules (lines) as structural elements
  • Typographic hierarchy through weight/size only
  • Generous whitespace as design element
  • Asymmetric layout (label column + content)
  • Uppercase micro-labels
  • Flat, honest surfaces
